## Exam Information

### NEET PG Exam Pattern
- **Total Questions:** 200
- **Total Marks:** 800
- **Marking Scheme:** +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect
- **Duration:** 3 hours 30 minutes
- **Mode:** Computer-based test (CBT)

### Exam Schedule (Recent Years)
- **2025:** August 3, 2025
- **2024:** August 11, 2024
- **2023:** March 2023
- **2022:** May 2022
- **2021:** September 2021
